📄 A Busy Saturday

Every Saturday, Emma and her brother Tom help their mom at home. In the morning, Emma likes to cook breakfast for the family. She makes eggs and toast. Then, Tom uses a broom to sweep the kitchen floor. After breakfast, they do the dishes together and then dry the dishes with a big towel.
